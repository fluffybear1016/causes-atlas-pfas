# Project Miami — PFAS Exposure Dashboard

Single-file Miami-Dade PFAS exposure dashboard for parents. Primary-source-cited end to end.

**Live URL:**
https://htmlpreview.github.io/?https://github.com/fluffybear1016/causes-atlas-pfas/blob/main/index.html

## What it covers

- Miami-Dade tap-water PFAS by utility (WASD, Miami Beach, NMB, REDAVO, South Dade) — pulled from each utility's own 2024 Annual Water Quality Report
- ZIP-code-level picker for nine Miami-Dade areas
- The peer-reviewed body-effect literature (Di Nisio 2019, Wang 2023, Roth 2025, Khalil 2016, Lopez-Espinosa 2016, Levine 2023, et al.)
- 10 of 13 PFAS-linked pediatric conditions rising in Miami-Dade
- The four DEP-confirmed Miami-Dade fire-training PFAS sites (4 of 4 assessed came back positive)
- The Miami-Dade County $199M AFFF MDL settlement (Resolution R-1015-23, Nov 7, 2023)
- The verbatim "excellent / safe" reassurance language vs the federal-court admissions
